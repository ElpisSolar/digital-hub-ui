About

Our vision is to provide free digital content to those who have no access to the internet.


You are accessing this digital-hub through a Raspberry Pi, a small computer, contained within all of our solar-powered units. It has an integrated Wi-Fi module through which we can create a Wireless Access Point (WAP). Using the WAP, we are able to turn this low cost computer into a server, and thereby can provide a host of digital content to nearby mobile phone users such as yourself.


What brings it all together is the digital hub, a user-interface that allows people to access the content stored on our devices through their mobile phones. So far we have deployed Arabic digital-hubs across refugee camps in Greece containing informational guides on mental health, women's healthcare, and a library of e-books, ranging from educational textbooks to childrenâ€™s books. Our goal is to gather and deploy content for all geographical contexts, and work closely with users to provide the most useful content possible.
